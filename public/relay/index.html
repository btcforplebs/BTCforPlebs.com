How to add the Relay

Insert the relay URL below into the "Relays" settings of your preferred Nostr client.

wss://relay.btcforplebs.com

Below are some quick steps for popular clients.

  • Nostr Relay Web UI: Add “wss://relay.btcforplebs.com” then tick “Enable”.
  • Nostrudel: Settings → Relays → Add Relay → paste URL → Save.
  • Other Apps (Nostrify, AT, etc.): Typically share an "Add Relay" or “Relay URL” field in the settings.
