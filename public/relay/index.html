TOS? Because We're Kind of Legit

By using our relay you consent to the following:

  1. I can't guarantee that the relay will be online -- it's a bit like a party in a haunted house: sometimes the lights go out.
  2. All messages are public and broadcast to (nearly) every relay in the network unless you explicitly set privacy on your client.
  3. Do not post illegal nonsense or sensitive personal info; your cat's birthday is fine though.
  4. We are not responsible for lost keys, spam, or that one spammer who keeps following and unfollwing for attention.
  5. If you're still a kid just let your mom run the server.

Feel free to start a chat with us if you're confused. We may not laugh at your puns, but we will keep your notes as safe as we can.
